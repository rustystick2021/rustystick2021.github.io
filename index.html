Who We Are

The Leading IT Startup

RustyStick Technologies began in 2020 as a consultancy focused on providing Engineering and Development in Outsystems low code platform to some of world's leading technology companies, start-ups and innovative enterprise organizations. The RustyStick Technologies approach was conceived through a recognised need to drive true enablement through technology. Far from simply equipping customers with the latest technologies to move their business forwards, we aim to position them to continuously improve through knowledge sharing and building internal capability.

RustyStick Technologies team consists of highly experienced agile technology advocates working across a breadth of industry sectors. We partner with customers to enable them to move quickly whilst achieving valuable change in their technology adoption and software development practices across their business.
